Avoid the Debt Collector by driving your train across space!
📖 STORY: THE RED PORTFOLIO
After a "guaranteed" intergalactic stock tip crashed, our protagonist was left drowning in cosmic debt. Desperate, he secured a loan from the ruthless mega-corporation, E Inc. Instead of paying his debts, he gambled the loan on the market again—and lost it all.

Now, E Inc. is coming to repossess his prized antique galactic train as collateral. Refusing to surrender, our protagonist does the only logical thing: he fires up the rusty, ancient engine and makes a daring, high-speed run for the stars to outrun his “TRAINTENEER”.

🚄 ABOUT THE GAME
Trainteneer is a dynamic, fast-paced endless runner arcade game where you command a high-speed spaceship-train trying to survive a track packed with deadly obstacles. In this cosmic ride, your ultimate goal is to keep your train head and all its attached carriages from being blown to pieces!

The longer your train, the more max health you have. But beware: every carriage that breaks away deals massive structural damage to your train's safety. Keep moving, think fast, shoot down hazards, and stay one step ahead of the Debt Collector's wrath!

🛠️ KEY FEATURES
Modular Upgrade System: Collect space coins during your run and invest them at the Upgrade Station to beef up your train's layout.
Laser Weaponry: Blast and destroy incoming asteroids and satellites blocking your path before you crash into them.
Defense System: Permanently reinforce your train's chassis to boost your Maximum HP.


⚡ POWER-UPS (CONFISCATED GOODS)
Grab these items on the track to turn the tide:

👻 Ghost Mode: Your train initiates a phase-shift! Become temporarily invincible and pass safely through deadly hazards without a single scratch.
🔥 Dual Laser: Double your firepower! Unleash a twin-beam barrage to quickly obliterate tougher obstacles in your path.
🚃 Carriage Replenish: Lost a few wagons along the way? Grab this power-up to instantly attach a new carriage and regain your crucial Max Health!
		


🕹️ GAME CONTROLS
[Swipe Up] : Jump / Dodge Upwards
[Swipe Down}: Fast Fall / Descend Quickly


🧩 THEME CORRELATION
Why it is "Absurd but Cool" The game takes a stressful, real-life adult problem—running out of money and hiding from a Debt Collector—and solves it in the craziest way possible: stealing a space-train armed with lasers! It is a hilarious idea mixed with awesome, high-speed action.
How it fits the "Inner Childhood Dream" Playing this game feels like taking your favorite toy trains and toy spaceships and smashing them together. The way the train wiggles up and down makes you feel like a kid again, guiding a giant toy train through a cosmic space playground
